Monster Realm — HD-2D lighting

A tiny lit tilemap. The hero and a wild Emberkit (wandering the tall-grass corner) are normal-mapped sprites; move under the lights and watch the shading react in real time — albedo + normal maps + a 2-light deferred pass, the pure-2D HD-2D look.
WASD / arrows — walk the hero
move the mouse over the scene — the cool light follows the cursor
Space — toggle day / night ambient
T — torch follows hero on/off
N — flip normal-map Y (if relief looks inverted)
V — flip normal sampling (if lighting is on the wrong half)
